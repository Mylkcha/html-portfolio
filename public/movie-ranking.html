The Best Movies According to Mycha

My top 3 movies of all-time

Macquia

This movie makes me feel melancholic. It showcases the different struggles and happiness of child rearing on the perspective of an entity who lives much longer than a human.

Howl's Moving Castle

Beautiful graphics and many foreshadowing. I love the element of fantasy.

Wicked

An absolute labor of love from the crew. Very faithful to the original broadway play.
